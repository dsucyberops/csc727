## Overview

This repository contains all supplementary research materials for *Project Phantom*, a structured literature review and comparative analysis examining deepfake technologies across three dimensions: technical detection, cybersecurity risk, and governance and ethics. It is organized to support reproducibility, transparency, and further research.

## Research Questions

**RQ1.** What ethical challenges arise from the development and use of deepfake technologies, particularly regarding consent, identity protection, and human dignity?

**RQ2.** What cybersecurity and fraud risks are introduced by deepfake technologies, and how effective are current detection approaches?

Both questions are answered in Section 10 (Discussion and Findings) of the paper.
